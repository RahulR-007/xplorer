Taj Mahal

3D Explore

Description and Review

📍 Location:

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, India, on the banks of the Yamuna River.

🏛️ Historical Significance:

Built between 1632 and 1653 by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan, the Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It was constructed as a **mausoleum for Mumtaz Mahal**, the emperor’s beloved wife, as a **symbol of his eternal love**.

🏗️ Architectural Marvel:

  • Designed in the Mughal architectural style, the Taj Mahal is known for its white marble exterior, symmetrical design, and intricate inlays of semi-precious stones.
  • The main structure features a massive dome, surrounded by four slender minarets.
  • It reflects different hues at different times of the day—pinkish in the morning, white in the afternoon, and golden under moonlight.

🛕 Interior Details:

  • The interior houses **cenotaphs of Mumtaz Mahal and Shah Jahan**, while their real graves lie in a lower crypt.
  • The walls are adorned with **calligraphy from the Quran** and intricate floral carvings.

🌍 Cultural & Touristic Importance:

  • The **Taj Mahal attracts millions of visitors annually** from around the world.
  • It is **one of India's most visited monuments**, symbolizing love, art, and history.

⭐ Fun Facts:

  • ✅ Took 22 years to complete with over 20,000 artisans.
  • ✅ The white marble was sourced from Makrana, Rajasthan.
  • ✅ Myth: Some legends say **Shah Jahan wanted to build a black Taj Mahal** opposite to this one, but no evidence supports it.
