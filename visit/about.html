About X-Plorer

Welcome to X-Plorer, your intelligent travel companion!

X-Plorer is designed to redefine how tourists experience destinations. With immersive 3D visuals, personalized recommendations, and a smart chatbot assistant, you can explore the wonders of the world like never before.

Whether you're interested in historical facts, architectural brilliance, cultural relevance, or fun trivia, X-Plorer presents it all in one seamless platform. Powered by modern web technology and Firebase-based authentication, it's built to scale and serve with ease.

Built with ❤️ using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Managed by an intuitive agent dashboard to keep the content fresh and evolving.
